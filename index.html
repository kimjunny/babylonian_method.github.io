Event Date Description
Big Bang 13.8 billion years ago The universe begins to expand and cool rapidly.
Formation of the first stars and galaxies 400-500 million years after the Big Bang The first stars and galaxies form from the cooling gas in the universe.
Formation of the Milky Way galaxy 12-13 billion years ago The Milky Way galaxy begins to form from gas and dust in the universe.
Formation of the Solar System 4.6 billion years ago The Solar System forms from a cloud of gas and dust.
Development of life on Earth 3.5 billion years ago The first life forms on Earth, most likely single-celled organisms.
Evolution of Homo sapiens 300,000 years ago Homo sapiens, the modern human, evolves in Africa.
