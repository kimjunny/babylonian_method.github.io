Chronological Tables of the Universe and Geological Periods

Table 1: History of the Universe

Era Period Event
Formation Big Bang
Stelliferous Era Formation of stars and galaxies
Degenerate Era White dwarfs and black dwarfs
Black Hole Era Black holes become dominant
Dark Era The universe becomes dark and cold

Table 2: Chronological Table of Geological Periods

Era Period Events
Paleozoic Cambrian Explosion of life, first appearance of complex organisms
Ordovician First land plants, diversification of marine life
Silurian First land animals, coral reefs
Devonian Age of fishes, first forests
Carboniferous Coal-forming swamps, amphibians dominate
Permian Mass extinction, rise of reptiles
Mesozoic Triassic First dinosaurs, rise of mammals
Jurassic Age of dinosaurs, first birds
Cretaceous Mass extinction, rise of flowering plants
Cenozoic Paleogene Age of mammals, first primates
Neogene Appearance of modern animals, including elephants and horses
Quaternary Appearance of humans, ice ages
